Instructions

Welcome to the Shell Spin the Wheel game! Here’s how you play:

  1. Fill in your name and phone number on the form.
  2. Click on "Entrer" to go to the spinning wheel page.
  3. Click on the "Tenter ma chance" button to spin the wheel.
  4. Wait for the wheel to stop to see if you’ve won a prize.

Good luck!
